Bengal Tiger

The Bengal Tiger is one of the largest members of most common species of Tiger.

  • Sceientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris
  • Average weight: 397 to 569 lb
  • Average Life: 8 to 10 years
  • Habitat: Mangroves, upland forests

Yellow to light orange color that in the belly and the internal areas of the legs becomes white or cream. Black, gray or brown stripes run vertically down all its body before the tail, where they become rings.
